# Analysis Questions Answered by the Dashboard

This document outlines the key analytical questions that the Virat Kohli Century Analytics Dashboard addresses. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to explore Virat Kohli's century-scoring performances across various dimensions.

## 1. Format-Based Analysis

Understanding how Virat Kohli's centuries are distributed across different cricket formats helps identify his strengths in each format.

- How are Virat Kohli's centuries distributed across Test, ODI, and T20 formats?
- In which format has he scored the highest number of centuries?
- Which format contributes the largest share of his total centuries?
- What is the average score of his centuries in each format?
- How does the frequency of centuries vary between formats?

## 2. Opposition Analysis

Analyzing performance against different opponents reveals patterns in Kohli's success against various teams.

- Against which teams has Virat Kohli scored the most centuries?
- Which opponents appear most frequently in his century list?
- Are there specific teams against whom he consistently performs well?
- What is the average century score against top opponents?
- How does his performance vary against traditional rivals vs. other teams?

## 3. Venue Analysis

Venue-based analysis can highlight the impact of home advantage and specific ground conditions on performance.

- At which venues or stadiums has Virat Kohli scored the most centuries?
- Do certain venues appear repeatedly in his century records?
- Is there a noticeable pattern in home vs away performances?
- Which continents or regions have seen the most centuries?
- How do venue-specific factors influence century frequency?

## 4. Time-Based Trends

Tracking performance over time helps understand career progression and peak periods.

- How has Virat Kohli's century-scoring trend changed over the years?
- Which year had the highest number of centuries?
- Are there periods where his performance peaked significantly?
- What is the monthly/seasonal distribution of his centuries?
- How has his century-scoring rate evolved throughout his career?

## 5. Performance Distribution

Examining the distribution of centuries provides insights into consistency and versatility.

- What percentage of his centuries come from each cricket format?
- How evenly are his centuries distributed across different opponents?
- What is the range and distribution of century scores?
- How many centuries were scored in winning vs. losing causes?
- What is the distribution of centuries by innings (1st vs. 2nd)?

## 6. Overall Performance Insights

Synthesizing data to draw broader conclusions about Kohli's career and dominance.

- What patterns can be identified in Virat Kohli's century-scoring performances?
- How can data visualization help reveal insights about his consistency and dominance in cricket?
- What are the key milestones in his century-scoring journey?
- How does his century record compare to other legendary batsmen?
- What factors contribute most to his century-scoring success?

## Dashboard Visualizations

The Power BI dashboard includes interactive charts and graphs to answer these questions:

- Pie charts for format distribution
- Bar charts for opponent and venue analysis
- Line charts for time-based trends
- Histograms for score distributions
- Maps for geographical analysis
- KPI cards for key statistics

These visualizations allow users to filter and drill down into specific aspects of Kohli's century performances for deeper insights.
